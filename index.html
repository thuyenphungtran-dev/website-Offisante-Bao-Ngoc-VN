Khoi dau tu nhung nam 1989, giua nhip song hoi ha cua Sai Gon xua, Thien Duyen Y Quan ra doi khong chi voi tu cach la mot hieu thuoc Dong Y, ma la noi gui gam niem hy vong cua nhung manh doi can duoc chua lanh.

Chung toi goi moi goi thuoc la mot "ban tinh ca cua thao moc", noi moi vi thuoc Bac, vi thuoc Nam duoc tuyen chon ky luong bang doi tay cua nhung bac thay tam huyet. Tai Bao Ngoc, chung toi khong "ban" thuoc, chung toi trao di su an tam.

"Y duc lam goc - Thien tam lam dau" khong chi la khau hieu, do la hoi tho trong tung cong thuc thuoc Luc Vi, Binh Vi ma ban cam tren tay hom nay.
